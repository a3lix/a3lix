# Our Story

Felix came from Freiburg. Marco grew up in Mississauga. They met working the line at a downtown hotel kitchen and spent three years arguing about whether pork or veal made a better schnitzel.

They never settled it. So they opened a restaurant.

Schnitzel Fri3nds is our love letter to two places at once — the Gasthaus warmth of southern Germany and the neighbourly ease of a Toronto side street. We breadcrumb by hand. We source our pork from a family farm outside Guelph. The lager is made with Bellwoods and is only available here.

We keep the menu short and rotate the specials weekly. Sit down, stay a while. The second round is always better than the first.

**Felix & Marco**
