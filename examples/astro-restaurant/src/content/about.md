# Our Story

Felix came from Freiburg. Marco grew up in Jonquière. They met working the line at a hotel in Montréal and spent three years arguing about whether pork or veal made a better schnitzel.

They never settled it. So they opened a restaurant in Chicoutimi.

Schnitzel Fri3nds is our love letter to two places at once — the Gasthaus warmth of southern Germany and the joual-accented ease of a Saguenay side street. We breadcrumb by hand. We source our pork from a family farm outside Saint-Honoré. The lager is brewed locally and is only available here.

We keep the menu short and rotate the specials weekly. *Asseyez-vous, restez un moment.* The second round is always better than the first.

**Felix & Marco**
